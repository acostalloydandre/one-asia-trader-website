Banana Chips

Product Description

Queens Honey Dipped Banana Chips are delightfuly crisp slices of premium Philippine bananas, delicately coated with natural sweetener for a perfect balance of sweetness and crunch. Each chip is carefuly fried to a golden perfection, offering a satisfying snack that’s both wholesome and indulgent. Made with 100% real bananas, these chips are free from artificial flavors and preservatives — a naturaly sweet treat you can enjoy anytime, anywhere

Key Features

  • Made from freshly harvested, localy grown bananas
  • Lightly dipped in natural sweetener for a rich, golden glaze
  • Crispy texture with a naturaly sweet aroma and flavor
  • No artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives
  • Perfect as a healthy on-the-go snack

Taste Profile

Sweet and fruity with a distinct honey aroma and a crunchy, satisfying texture.

Ingredients

Saba banana, water, refined sugar, honey, natural flavoring, coconut oil.

Allergen Information

Contains no known food allergen.

Storage Instruction

Store in a cool dry place, away from direct sunlight.

Direction for Use

Great Snack helps you refuel on your activities. Rich in Potassium.
