Our Company

One Asia Trader History

Founded in year 2006, One Asia Trader has been a steadfast symbol of HOPE, RESILIENCE AND COMMUNITY SUPPORT. Although the company was not fully operational in its early years, we retained our name and vision, believing that someday our efforts would contribute significantly to prosperity and social betterment.

Our Mission

At One Asia Trader, our mission is to create sustainable job opportunities, promote local products to the international market and support less fortunate children through our business endeavors.

Our Vision

To become a distinguished international trading company known for quality, integrity and sustainability.

Why “Queens” is our logo? Because it symbolizes strength, dignity, and the noble pursuit of turning a dream into reality. It reflects our commitment to empowering our community and elevating local products globally, with grace and purpose.

Our Commitment

What started as a dream, fueled by love and perseverance has become a mission to serve with a big heart. Our focus remains unwavering- to build a business that not only thrives economically but also uplifts the lives of those less fortunate children.



Join us. Together, we turn dreams into reality - One step, One product, One life at a time.
